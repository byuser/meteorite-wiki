About This Wiki

The Meteorite Wiki is a small, self-contained educational website that introduces the main types of meteorites, what they are made of, and how they are classified.

What you'll find here

The wiki is organised into a handful of pages: an introduction, a browsable gallery of meteorite types, a breakdown of their composition, a guide to classification, and a tour of some famous meteorites.

How it's built

This site is intentionally simple: plain HTML, CSS and a little JavaScript, with no build step or external dependencies. All of the meteorite content lives in a single shared data file so the same information powers every page and is checked by an automated test suite.

A note on accuracy

Content here is summarised for learning and is not a substitute for peer-reviewed sources. For authoritative data, see organisations such as the Meteoritical Society and its Meteoritical Bulletin Database.
